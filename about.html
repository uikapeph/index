What is Uikapeph?

Drinking Café is a Tradition to us and It becomes A part of our Daily Lives, this is why the marketers intend to Open a Café shop in some Places. We all know That Café is a Very Famous Drink in the Philippines It is One of the most needed drinks of some adults, it had beena habit for some Filipinos. We want our café to be known, so as a Filipino we always Say the word "UI" or "Huy" In a sentence like we are calling or we want something like "Ui Ganda" Then we start to think that it is very Unique and It is very Good to our ear if We heard Our customer says "Ui Tara Kape" And It is Located or address in Philippines so We put The Initials of our Country. And We want to startasmall business selling Cafe Named "UI KAPE PH".

At some point,in our Time Some Café has been Upgraded It have many Flavors and Many kinds of Café. It is nice to have a business that is Unique and Have Originality. "Ui Kape Ph" will be one-of-a-kind, we want to provide our customers With a Good quality and Delicious Café That they will going to taste. Since It's Pandemic and We Are still starting, the shop will be located at the house of our CEO in Cambria Bay Blk:12 Lot:52 Laguna. In our Café House You Will Feel at Home and Some Customers can easily Access the Address of our Café because It is near at The South Los Banos.
